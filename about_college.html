About Us

Sri Venkateswara Polytechnic (SVPT) is a well-established technical institute located in Etcherla, Srikakulam, Andhra Pradesh. Approved by AICTE and affiliated with SBTET, the college has earned a solid reputation for providing quality diploma education since its inception. It offers full-time engineering diploma programs that cater to the academic and career aspirations of students after completing their 10th standard.

SVPT offers diploma courses in five major engineering branches: Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Electronics & Communication. These programs are carefully designed to align with current industry needs and are taught by highly experienced faculty. The college has a seat capacity of 420, ensuring wide access to affordable education.

One of SVPT’s highlights is its strong infrastructure, which includes fully furnished laboratories, a digital library, modern classrooms, and separate hostel facilities for students. There is also a well-organized transportation system covering nearby areas, making commuting easy and safe. In addition, the campus promotes discipline and academic focus, with rules such as compulsory attendance, a dress code, and mobile restrictions during class hours.

To support holistic development, SVPT organizes regular personality development programs, Career Readiness Training (CRT) sessions, and study hours for hostel students. Students are also trained in industry-relevant technologies like AutoCAD, CNC, PLC, and SCADA. Through such initiatives, the institute ensures that students don’t just gain theoretical knowledge but also develop practical skills for the job market.

The college hosts a variety of cultural and academic events throughout the year, including freshers’ day, Vinayaka Chavathi Nimajanam, farewell functions, and Sankranthi Sambaralu. With over 5+ clubs and more than 10+ annual events, students get ample opportunities to explore their interests and build lasting memories beyond the classroom.

Each year, more than 250 students are placed in reputable companies, with Infosys among the top recruiters. Backed by a dedicated placement cell and alumni success stories, SVPT stands as a symbol of opportunity, growth, and empowerment through technical education. It is not just a college—it’s a launchpad for future engineers and innovators.

Sri Venkateswara Polytechnic takes pride in nurturing not only academic excellence but also leadership and innovation among its students. Various student-led initiatives and club activities provide platforms for showcasing talents, team-building, and project-based learning. The emphasis on co-curricular growth ensures that students graduate with the confidence and skills needed in today’s competitive world.

The administration at SVPT is student-centric, maintaining a transparent and responsive approach. Regular interactions between faculty and students foster a supportive academic environment. Feedback mechanisms, mentorship, and academic counseling help guide students at every stage of their diploma journey, building a bridge between student aspirations and institutional support.
