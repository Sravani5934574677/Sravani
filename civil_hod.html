About HOD Of Civil Engineering

Sri. G. Achyuth is the esteemed Head of the Civil Engineering Department at Sri Venkateswara Polytechnic. With a solid academic foundation holding an M.Tech degree and over a decade of professional experience, he leads the department with vision, discipline, and academic integrity. His expertise covers structural analysis, construction planning, surveying, and sustainable building technologies.

As a mentor and educator, Mr. Achyuth has significantly contributed to shaping the academic paths of numerous diploma students, equipping them with both theoretical grounding and practical skills. He emphasizes experiential learning through lab work, field projects, and industry visits, ensuring that students gain hands-on knowledge of civil engineering practices.

Under his leadership, the Civil Department has seen major growth in student project initiatives, modern lab facilities, and innovative classroom practices. He plays a pivotal role in aligning the curriculum with the needs of modern infrastructure development and government standards, preparing students for careers in both the public and private sectors.

Mr. Achyuth also ensures student involvement in extracurricular and co-curricular activities, fostering teamwork, problem-solving, and project management skills. His encouragement of interdisciplinary learning helps civil engineering students understand how their work integrates with environmental and urban planning concerns.
