At the helm of Sri Venkateswara Polytechnic is our esteemed Principal, Palavalasa Bupal Rao—a visionary academician and a strong advocate for value-based technical education. With decades of experience in the field of engineering education, he has been instrumental in steering the institution toward excellence and relevance in an ever-evolving technological world. His leadership style is rooted in discipline, innovation, and student-centered development, making him not only an administrator but also a mentor and guide to both faculty and students. Mr. Bupal Rao firmly believes that knowledge is the foundation of true prosperity and that education must go beyond textbooks to shape responsible, confident, and self-reliant individuals. His mission is to develop students who possess not just technical expertise, but also integrity, empathy, and a sense of civic responsibility. Under his leadership, SVPT has embraced a culture of continuous learning, modern teaching methods, and industry-aligned training. One of his core philosophies is that character building is as important as skill building. He often emphasizes that strong moral values and clear vision are key ingredients for lasting success. To this end, he ensures that the college environment fosters not just academic excellence, but also personal discipline, mutual respect, and ethical behavior. Our Principal has championed various initiatives at SVPT including curriculum enrichment, faculty development, digital infrastructure enhancement, and holistic student grooming. He has introduced industry-relevant certifications, soft skill training, and mentorship programs that bridge the gap between education and employment. He takes special interest in the academic and emotional well-being of hostel students and supports co-curricular engagements that bring out creativity and leadership. A believer in leading by example, Mr. Bupal Rao is known for his punctuality, dedication, and accessibility. He frequently interacts with students through regular feedback sessions, counseling hours, and institutional events, providing them with guidance and encouragement. His open-door policy and approachable demeanor have made him a respected figure among students, parents, and staff alike. Through his untiring efforts, SVPT has grown not just in numbers, but in stature—recognized for its disciplined campus, skilled graduates, and positive learning environment. He envisions the institution as a launchpad for future engineers, innovators, and changemakers who will contribute to society and industry with purpose and pride. In essence, Principal Palavalasa Bupal Rao embodies the values SVPT stands for—knowledge, growth, discipline, and service. His unwavering commitment to student success continues to inspire every member of the institution to strive for excellence, making SVPT not just a college, but a transformative experience for all who enter its gates.
